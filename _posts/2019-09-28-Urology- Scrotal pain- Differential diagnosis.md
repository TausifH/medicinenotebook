> REMEMBER: Pain in the scrotum could also include pain in the testes. 

- Testicular torsion
- Epididymo-orchitis
- Inguinal hernia
- Haematocele
- Epididymal cyst/spermatocele
- Testicular cancer
- Squamous cell carcinoma of the scrotum
- Varicocele (rarely painful)
- Hydrocele (rarely painful)
